Narendra Modi to address IT, telecom cos on February 7

NEW DELHI: Within a month of his round-table meeting with IT and telecom firms, Narendra Modi will address companies in the sector on February 7 through a video link.

"He (Modi) will speak on digital entrepreneurship and digital economy," an invitation by Internet And Mobile Association of India (IAMAI) said.

Modi in his last interaction with the sectoral players in January laid emphasis on pushing electronics manufacturing, use of technology in reforming agriculture and education.

IAMAI is chaired By Google India's Managing Director Rajan Anandan. Google has earlier organised online video chat of Modi in August 2012 on its then new service hangout.

The Gujarat Chief Minister again used Hangout technology for speaking at Google Big Tent Activate Summit in 2013.
