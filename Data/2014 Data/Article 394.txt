Aam Aadmi Party's election manifesto to focus on good governance, Jan Lokpal Bill and corruption

LUCKNOW: Good governance, Jan Lokpal Bill and corruption would be main focus of the Aam Aadmi Party's election manifesto, the party today said.

Incharge of manifesto drafting committee of AAP Adarsh Shastri said the party would finalise its manifesto after taking the suggestions of the people.Shastri said that they would take suggestions on the issues of Jan Lokpal bill, good governance and clean politics.

He told reporters that issues of women empowerment, unemployment, agri policy and small and medium industries would also be included in the manifesto.Shastri said that the people have to face problems related to power, water and traffic and these daily issues would be given a prominent place in the manifesto of AAP.
